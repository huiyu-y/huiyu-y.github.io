Poster, July 2022. 29.7 x 42 cm

Peace is the soft clouds, the quietness of blue, the moisture of the rain, and the comfort and usual days. Greedy and war covertly hide behind the pacific days that we take for granted. I'm not sure if you have noticed that the shapes of the clouds are the word “peace” spelled in the Morse code, which is widely used during the war. All the dots and dashes are burnt holes of paper. The blue covers the burnt color of the edges. The raindrop graphics cross out the dates of the Ukraine war Russo-Ukrainian War. The war is still occurring after we sign numerous declarations of peace.

2022 VCUarts Thesis Show

Multi-platform design, July 2022.

Loop GIFs, 00:12 / Ink Print(silver/black), double sided, 10 × 17 in / Vinyl on Wall

The main concept of this design is to create an inclusive design for every artist in this show. I lay out each student's name and hand draw the shape created by the negative space between their names. All participants and departments are emphasized equally, and every student is the main element that composes this design. The thesis show is the most important exhibition that shows the art school students' hard work during the two years. We are all familiar with the experience that we have to work night and day for deadlines. So I use the transition of the black and white color to visualize these shared memories.

Voice

72.8 x 103 cm.

Not until I left my home country that I realize how voice can bring a sense of belonging. When I felt homesick in the United States, I would listen to the recording of the daily conversation between myself and people in my home country, and I could imagine I was at home. Voice is about our experience, culture, thoughts, and language. This poster of the graphical notation is created to record the voices around me that I grew up with and to make these voices tangible to every viewer.The main materials of the poster are four recordings of the daily conversation between myself and the people in my neighborhood. It is designed to be B1 size with an ivory background like the Suzuki sheet music and green notations that stands for the Taiwan image in my mind.
