The Random Stone

Artist book, September 2021.
Risograph, Frame by Frame Drawing,
5.5 × 8.5 in.

This casette is a music video for the random stone I picked up after my 8:30 p.m. class.

The way to enjoy this piece is open for the audience to explore and define. The audiences are welcome to read it, listen to it, or feel it with their hands. All the drawings on the case are exactly the life-size of the stone. This cassette tape is not used to hold audio but the drawings of the stone. The twigs and small fruit put in the case are the sound sources for this piece. This works seeks to experiment with the system and function of the obsolete medium with a mundane object.
