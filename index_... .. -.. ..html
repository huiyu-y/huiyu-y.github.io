Huiyu Yang is a designer from Taiwan based in New York, US, currently work as junior designer at Wolff Olins. Her design practices as adapting but challenges the existing system in both design and society. She works across various mediums and genres including printed matter, installations, websites and motion pictures. In 2022, she graduated with an MFA from Virginia Commonwealth University. She previously completed a master's in Motion Media Design from Savannah College of Arts and Design.

Inquiries about freelance work or collaborative projects, feel free to reach out to me.
