2022 VCUarts Thesis Show

Exhibition Identity, July 2022.

Loop GIFs, 00:12 / Ink Print(silver/black), double sided, 10 × 17 in / Vinyl on Wall

The main concept of this exhibition identity is to create an inclusive design for every artist in this show. I lay out each student’s name and hand draw the shape created by the negative space between their names. All participants and departments are emphasized equally, and every student is the main element that composes this design.

The thesis show is the most important exhibition that shows the art school students’ hard work during the two years. We are all familiar with the experience that we have to work night and day for deadlines. So I use the transition of the black and white color to visualize these shared memories.
